About Our Legacy

Driving Excellence Since 1968

Located in the heart of India's automotive hub, Bara Bazaar, Kashmere Gate, Rajasthan Auto Enterprises has been a cornerstone in the auto parts industry for over five decades.

As an Authorized Mahindra & Mahindra spare parts dealer, we pride ourselves on delivering 100% genuine components. Whether you are a retailer looking for bulk wholesale supplies or an individual seeking precise parts for your vehicle, our expansive inventory and expert staff ensure you get exactly what you need.

  • Comprehensive range of Mahindra & Mahindra OEM parts.
  • Competitive pricing for both Wholesale and Retail clients.
  • Dedicated support team with decades of technical expertise.
